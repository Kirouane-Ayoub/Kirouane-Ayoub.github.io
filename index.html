The detection system typically uses image processing techniques and machine learning algorithms to analyze images of rice leaves and identify signs of disease. In the case of Bacterial Leaf Blight, the system may look for characteristic yellowing or necrosis of the leaves. For Blast, the system may detect lesions or spots on the leaves or sheaths. For Brown spot, the system may identify small, dark spots on the leaves.

Pixelated Privacy: A Face Blur Project for Anonymizing Images

Face blur is a technique used to protect the privacy of individuals in images or videos. It involves blurring or obscuring the facial region of a person to prevent their identification. Face blur is commonly used in situations where individuals may have a reasonable expectation of privacy, such as in CCTV footage, social media posts, or news broadcasts.
